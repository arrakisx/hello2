 IrfanView is an old photo viewer used by many users reliably and easily. Its developer has made it very useful even for new versions of Operating Systems. You can have many new and practical features. It is a freeware photo viewer.
It is very fast and opens your images quickly and instantly without any delay. It can optimize your photos, resize and rotate images. IrfanView has basic editing tools like cropping, resizing, red-eye removal or rotating of images. Create slides show and print your photos via IrfanView. Batch processing is also supported in it. IrfanView not only supports image formats but also play audio and video formats. It is available in multi languages.
